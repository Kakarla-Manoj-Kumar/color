üí° Pro Tip Recap: Color Psychology Essentials

  • The 60-30-10 Rule: Use a dominant color (60%), a secondary color (30%), and an accent color (10%) for perfect balance.
  • Accent Colors: Use high-contrast, energetic colors (like Red or Bright Orange) exclusively for your most important CTAs.
  • Accessibility First: Always ensure sufficient contrast between your text and background colors (especially on dark themes) for legibility.
